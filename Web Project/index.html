  1. Important Supplies for Tennis:
    • Hard court shoes.
    • A visor.
    • Tennis racquet.
    • Tennis balls.
  2. Tips for Tennis Forehand:
    • Check that grip is correct.
    • Turn shoulders and plant feet.
    • Follow through shot and turn to face the net.
    • Swing from low to high.
    • Finish swing across body to opposite shoulder.
  3. Ideas for Mental Stamina:
    • Take a deep breath before serving.
    • Acknolwdge the mistake and learn from it.
    • Focus on the next point.
    • Always go back to the basics.
    • Smile at the small victories.
